
Chocolate Chip Brownies 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
 
4 squares of unsweetened chocolate
2 cups sugar
1 cup flour
3 eggs
2 sticks butter
1 t vanilla
1 6oz bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips
 
Melt Butter and chocolate.
Stir in sugar gradually.
Beat in eggs one at a time.
Add Vanilla
Stir in flour.
Grease and flour a 13 Â½ x 9 x 2 pan.  
Pour in batter.
Sprinkle chocolate chips over mix and bake for 30-35 minutes.
